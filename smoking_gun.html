2024 Early Voting in Clark County, NV - "Smoking Gun" Analysis

This visualization shows the rolling average of Trump votes for each voting machine. Unlike the game history view that shows cumulative percentages, this displays the average of the most recent K votes at each point, where K is the window size you can adjust with the slider.

A legitimate election would show random variations in the rolling average as votes are counted, following a pattern consistent with statistical noise. A manipulated election might show sudden jumps or systematic shifts in the rolling average beyond what would be expected from natural variation.
