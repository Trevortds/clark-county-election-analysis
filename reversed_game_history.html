Reversed Vote Counting Progression

This visualization shows the vote counting progression in reverse - starting from the final vote and working backwards to the first vote. Only tabulators with at least 250 total votes are included to focus on machines with significant voting history.

Each line represents a tabulator's vote counting journey in reverse chronological order. The visualization helps demonstrate how vote percentages appear when viewed backwards in time.
